Here and now

You will receive an instant payment for your direct partner. When your partner activates the company's product, you will be credited with income in internal MRT tokens. The income percentage varies depending on your level in the company: levels 1 and 2 - 3.5%, from level 3 to 6 - 4%, from level 7 to 8 - 4.5% and from level 9 to 10 - 5%. The level of income depends on the amount of opening a deposit of direct partners. From indirect partners you will receive dividend payments.

Dividends

You will receive income from cooperation on the same day as your direct partner from active products of the company. Dividends are paid in MRT from the beginning to the end of the partner's product, there is no instant payout. The percentage of income varies depending on your level in the company: levels 1 and 2 - 7%, from 3 to 6 levels - 8%, from 7 to 8 - 9% and from 9 to 10 levels - 10%. The level of income depends on the amount of opening a deposit of direct partners. From indirect partners you will receive dividend payments.

You can change the type of cooperation at levels 5 and 8.
